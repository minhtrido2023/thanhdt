## RUBBER prototype — VALIDATED but CONTRARIAN (`rubber_cyclical.py`)
Event study, 1,016 monthly obs across 6 rubber stocks, fwd 1Y/2Y by rubber regime (price vs trailing-36m median = GOOD/WEAK) × stock drawdown:
- **rubber WEAK + stock DEEP dd<−25% → 1Y +37%/win79%, 2Y +69%/win87%** (best). rubber WEAK any-dd: +18%/+37%. ALL: +7%/+21%. **rubber GOOD → 1Y −0%/2Y +2% (poor)**.
- **KEY: buy at the commodity TROUGH (price weak/depressed) + stock beaten down, NOT when commodity is strong/elevated.** Classic cyclical mean-reversion — by the time the commodity is clearly "good/uptrend," the upside is priced. This REFINES the user's "buy when commodity uptrend" → actually buy commodity-WEAK + stock-dislocated (the trough, before uptrend obvious).
- Stock dislocation is the DOMINANT driver; commodity-weak is an added tailwind. DRI face validity: 04/2025 Close 10,800/dd−33%/PB 1.23 in market crisis → +31% 1Y (rubber was elevated then — stock-cheapness carried it; intraday low 8,390 → +80%).
- **⚠️ CURRENT caution**: rubber at 95th pctile → rubber stocks at EXPENSIVE end of cycle → poor expected forward per this pattern. Good entries (rubber weak) were 2019-20, 2023 — passed. DRI already +80% from Apr-2025 low.
- Caveats: monthly autocorrelated obs; "GOOD→poor" partly driven by 2011 super-peak crash; IndexMundi monthly ≠ exact VN rubber price. Directional, not a clean backtest.
