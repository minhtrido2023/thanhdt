## CTR — Viettel Construction  ★ the find of the sweep
**Archetype.** Not a pure American-Tower/Crown-Castle tower-co (GPM only ~10% = construction-thin,
not tower high-margin). It's an **asset-light infra-services compounder**: telecom installation/construction
for the Viettel network + a growing tower-leasing book. Return profile is true-quality: **ROE_TTM 20→31→30%,
ROIC5Y 14.5→24.3→21.1%** (highest in the telecom group). FSCORE 6 (2026Q1). DY = 0 (retains all).

**Primary metric = EV/EBITDA.** And it works monotonically. Forward-12M return by EVEB entry bucket
(month-end sampling, prune EVEB-join, fwd LEAD 250 sessions):

| EVEB entry | n | avg fwd-12M | win% |
|-----------|---|-------------|------|
| **< 9**   | 23 | **+132.1%** | **100%** |
| 9–13      | 28 | +44.5%      | 89% |
| ≥ 13      | 29 | **−7.4%**   | 34% |

**Robust, not one episode** — EVEB<9 entries fired in 4 separate years incl. the bear:
2018 (n3,+152%), 2019 (n7,+109%), 2020 (n10,+163%), **2022 bear (n3,+64%)**. Buying CTR cheap on EV/EBITDA
has paid every time across regimes.

**Screen.** `EVEB < 9` (strong buy) or `< 11` (accumulate) **AND** `ROIC5Y > 20` **AND** `ROE_Trailing > 25`.
**Current (2026Q1): EVEB ~10.1 → mid-bucket (9–13, hist +44%/89%)** — accumulate, not the screaming <9 buy.
EVEB hasn't printed <9 since 2022.

**Verdict: CAPTURABLE.** Liquid (29bn ADV), 8y history, robust cross-regime valuation-timed signal.
This is a genuine book/watchlist single name — rare in this sweep, where almost everything was
lens-not-book or un-capturable. Treat as a valuation-timed quality compounder.

## VTP — Viettel Post
**Archetype.** Pitched as FedEx/UPS-early (e-commerce express, volume×price scale-up). **The thesis broke.**
The VN last-mile price war (J&T / Shopee-SPX / GHN / GHTK) crushed pricing: RevYoY 52→84→**110% (2021 peak)**
then collapse → **+12 (2022) → −17 (2023) → −2 (2024) → +8 (2025) → −6 (2026Q1)**. NPM thin & flat (~3-4%).
**ROIC5Y deteriorated 33→13%, ROE 47→21%.** A decelerator, not a compounder.

**Primary metric = P/S** (thin-margin scale-up; EBITDA depressed → EVEB structurally >13 ALWAYS, never gives
a buy: EVEB<9 n=0; 9–13 only at the 2023 max-pessimism trough n=7 → +139% bounce). P/S history:
**1.48 (2019) → 0.17 (2023Q1 trough) → 0.58 (2026Q1)**. The 0.17 deep-pessimism print was a real contrarian
buy — but it's a **single mean-reversion episode on a worsening fundamental**.

**Screen / verdict.** Mechanically capturable (16.5bn ADV, prune 2019) **but a quality/return screen correctly
REJECTS VTP** (falling ROIC & ROE). Only a deep-contrarian `P/S < 0.25` mean-reversion lens applies, and that's
rare + value-trap-prone. **Lesson: Viettel-halo (brand + state backing) ≠ quality** — the unit economics got
competed away. Lens-not-book, low conviction.

## TOS — Tân Cảng Logistics
**Archetype.** Port-adjacent logistics/warehousing (not ocean shipping). On fundamentals it's the **best
trajectory of the three** — a genuine asset-light improving compounder: RevYoY accelerating **37→58→73%**,
NPM expanding **10→20%**, GPM 20→30%, ROIC5Y rising 13→17%, **ROE_TTM 18→46%**, and CHEAP (EVEB 3.7–5.5,
PE 5.7–8.3). Primary metric EV/EBITDA + ROIC-trend.

**The wall = tradability.** ADV **1.08 bn/d** (avg vol ~6k shares) — a thin micro-cap, barely above the
1bn illiquidity floor; prune only since **2024-04 (446d)**; FSCORE just dropped to 2 (2026Q1 — watch).
Cannot be sized or backtested.

**Verdict: UN-CAPTURABLE for now.** Best economics, worst liquidity — like the pure-telecom names
(un-backtestable by recency) PLUS a micro-liquidity wall. Watchlist/lens. Proposed entry IF it deepens
(>3–5bn ADV sustained): `EVEB < 5 AND ROIC5Y rising AND NPM expanding`. Today: prune-flag ≠ tradable.
