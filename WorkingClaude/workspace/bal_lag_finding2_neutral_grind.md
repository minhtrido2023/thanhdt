1. **Momentum-of-momentum gate** (causal): daily cross-sectional momentum-factor return = top-quintile minus bottom-quintile `ta_mom`, realized t→t+1 (`workspace/mom_factor.sql` → `mom_factor.csv`); trailing 60/120/250d, shifted 1d.

2. **Universe-vs-index decomposition** (EW liquid-quality universe = the book's pond vs VNINDEX, monthly cum):

   ⇒ The broad quality universe **absolutely declined** 2025-09→2026 while the index rose on **megacaps (VIC-led)**. The book equal-weights the broad pond → it cannot capture a cap-weighted megacap rally.

4. **Momentum gradient by state** (why you can't just lower the threshold):
   - BULL: steep & monotone — ta≥155 +9.3% / ta 70-95 +2.8%. High threshold earns a big premium → keep it.
   - NEUTRAL: **flat plateau** — ta≥155 +4.3% / 140-155 +5.5% / 125-140 +4.7% / 95-125 +3.3% / 70-95 +2.4%. Above ~125 there is ~no premium; below ~95 it dilutes into noise (53% win). The winners (ta 50-130) live in/below the noise band.

**Momentum SELECTION criteria cannot fix the grind persistence.** Confirms the workspace prior: "momentum book RESISTS overlays." The only validated lever this session is Finding #1 (EXBULL onset).
