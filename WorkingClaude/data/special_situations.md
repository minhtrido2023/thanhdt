## DGC — Hóa chất Đức Giang (+ PAT, the apatite arm) — updated 2026-05-31

**Event (verified: Tuổi Trẻ / Người Quan Sát / Dân Trí, Apr 2026):**
- Khai trường 25 (Lào Cai apatite mine) SUSPENDED for investigation → DGC forced onto imported/external ore → yellow-phosphorus (P4) COGS up → Q1/26 GPM 28.9% (vs 34-35%).
- Chairman/founder **Đào Hữu Huyền + son (Đào Hữu Duy Anh) + 1 board member prosecuted & detained** (Bộ Công an) — alleged illegal mining + illegal waste dumping. Board re-elected; **founder family retained the chair role** (continuity).
- Q1/26: Rev −24%, NPAT −48.6% to 430bn (lowest since Q3-2021); operating CF −1,100bn (from +345bn).
- Note: NP also softened 4 quarters BEFORE the event (842→752→622→409bn) = cyclical demand softening + event spike-down.

**Bull case (user + moat analysis):** Q2 recovers vs Q1 (continuous ops, 1 mine reopened, higher selling prices); franchise IRREPLACEABLE in VN (dominant phosphorus producer, vertical integration + production know-how, stable customers, product sells out); leadership continuity (family); if legal penalty bounded (~2yr) → limited business impact. Classic Buffett "good business, one bad quarter."

**Bear / real risks (the swing variables — NOT which family member chairs):**
1. **MINE ACCESS = THE swing variable.** If Khai trường 25 access is permanently curtailed (illegal-mining angle), DGC loses raw-material self-sufficiency → STRUCTURAL margin hit, not temporary. Temporary suspension = recoverable; permanent loss = thesis breaks.
2. **Sulfur cost headwind** (see sulfur_anchors.csv): sulfur ~doubled ~$300→$600-700/t on Middle East war (main supply region); a key P4/phosphoric-acid input → margin pressure that persists even after mine reopens. DGC margin = f(P4 output price, sulfur input, apatite access) — squeezed on input from 2 sides.
3. Legal resolution timeline + reputational/regulatory overhang (uncertain duration).

**Watch triggers (re-rate to BUY when):** (a) Khai trường 25 access RESTORED (raw-material self-sufficiency back); (b) legal matter resolved/bounded; (c) sulfur price normalizes (ME war de-escalates). Verdict now: SPECIAL_SITUATION — higher-risk turnaround, NOT auto-AVOID nor clean buy; size for legal/mine uncertainty.

**Data gaps:** real P4 output price (no clean series — SunSirs image-only) and sulfur (anchors only, no monthly series). DGC margin model is qualitative until a paid feed (SunSirs/SMM) supplies P4 + sulfur monthly.
