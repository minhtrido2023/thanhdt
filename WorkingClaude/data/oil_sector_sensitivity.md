## PHAN 2 — Anh huong len LOI NHUAN (quarterly)
corr cua tung chi tieu voi Brent. NP=net profit (level), NPM=net margin, GPM=gross margin.
c_lvl = corr(metric, Brent_quy hien tai); c_l1/l2 = voi Brent tre 1/2 quy (do tre vong von/ton kho);
c_dNP = corr(NP YoY-ratio NP_R, Brent YoY) — bat hieu ung GIA dau TANG/GIAM (inventory).
